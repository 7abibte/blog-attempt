Welcome to Pau

This part describes what the blog is about. It tells that you’ll find fun things to do, tips and great ways to spend an afternoon, a weekend, or a getaway. You’ll learn about fun things to do in the city and within a 2 hour drive. Visit the mountains, head to the coast, learn to surf, go for a hike, watch the Tour de France in live action, check out the Grand Prix, and many other fun things.
